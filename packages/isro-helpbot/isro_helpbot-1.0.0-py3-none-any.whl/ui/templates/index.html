About ISRO HelpBot

Navigating the vast and complex MOSDAC and ISRO portals can be overwhelming for users seeking specific information about Indian space missions. The abundance of data and technical jargon often creates barriers to quick and accurate knowledge retrieval.

ISRO HelpBot leverages cutting-edge Generative AI technology to parse documents, extract relevant knowledge graphs, and respond to natural language queries in real time. This intelligent assistant empowers users to effortlessly explore Indian space research with contextual and precise answers.

How It Works

  1. 1

    User asks question

    Users type natural language queries about Indian space missions or ISRO data.

  2. 2

    Backend parses intent + queries LLM

    The FastAPI backend analyzes the question, extracts intent, and sends it to the language model.

  3. 3

    Knowledge Graph facts injected

    Relevant facts from the knowledge graph are combined with the LLM prompt for accurate context.

  4. 4

    Contextual answer returned

    The AI generates a precise, context-aware response delivered back to the user.

  5. 5

    User can upload new documents

    Users enrich the knowledge base by uploading PDFs, DOCX, or XLSX files directly through the chatbot.

RAG, Knowledge Graph & NLP Explained

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines large language models with external knowledge sources to generate accurate, context-aware answers.

Knowledge Graphs structure information as entities and relationships, enabling precise reasoning and fact injection.

NLP (Natural Language Processing) allows the system to understand and respond to user queries in natural, conversational language.
